Overview

This website is a final project for CTIS 230 Web Development. It is based of teachings in JAPAN 101 and 102 taught by Professor Hirakawa at Guilford College. It is meant to be a sort of cheat sheet for these classes and contains commonly forgotten alphabets,vocab, kanji and grammar rules. It even includes te form and past tense practice quizzes.

About the Creator

My name is Suvesh Bhusal and I have taken JAPAN 101 and 102 at Guilford College in past semesters. I am a student at the Early College at Guilford. I have a passion for learning Japanese, which is why I created this website.
